How to play

  • Tap or press Space to jump over grounded monsters.
  • Press ↓ (or swipe down on mobile) to duck under wraiths.
  • The game speeds up the longer you survive — score comes from distance and time.
  • Tap or press Space again after a crash to start a new run.

Reward rules

  • Paste the address you want $HOODIE sent to — no wallet connection or signature required.
  • You earn 1 $HOODIE per point, up to a cap of 50,000 $HOODIE for a single run.
  • Very short runs may not qualify — play a real run, not just a tap.
  • After a run ends, share your score on X, then claim — rewards are validated automatically and sent within seconds, batched with other players. No gas, no extra signature.
